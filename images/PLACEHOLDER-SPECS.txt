PeakRanking Digital Solutions: image specs

Current assets: hero.png, value-clarity.png, value-sustainability.png, value-authority.png, roadmap.png, cta.png (index.html); about.png, manny-pachano.png (about.html, leadership portrait).

If you replace them, keep the same filenames or update the src paths in index.html.
Preferred formats: WebP for photos (with JPG fallback if needed); SVG or WebP for flat graphics.
Keep file sizes reasonable (hero ideally under ~250KB after compression).

1) hero.*
   - Primary emotional anchor under the headline.
   - Aspect: ~16:9. Important content center-weighted (cropping on mobile).
   - Content ideas: Strategist with analytics, growth illustration, or brand-appropriate scene.
   - Style: Emerald (#0d6b4d) accents; avoid neon or clichéd stock tropes.

2) value-clarity.* | value-sustainability.* | value-authority.*
   - Deliverables / durable growth / authority & trust: three distinct but visually consistent images.
   - Aspect: ~4:3 or 1:1. Match lighting and color grade across all three.

3) roadmap.*
   - Supports the 12-month phases; timeline or phase diagram with four clear steps.
   - Aspect: wide banner; readable at desktop width.

4) cta.*
   - Humanizes the consultation block: people, workspace, or approachable results context.
   - Aspect: ~9:5 or 4:3; should work stacked on narrow screens.

When you export:
- Update <img src="…"> if filenames differ.
- Use descriptive alt text for each image.
