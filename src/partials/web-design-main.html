Better Load Times Lead To Higher Conversions

It’s tough out there online, and every little advantage counts. While your competition is dealing with page builders and sluggish load speeds, you could be running something completely different. Something hand-coded that loads the second someone taps it. This matters because if your site takes longer than 3 seconds to load, you can lose half your traffic right there. People just won’t stick around for it. Our sites load before anyone has a chance to get impatient, which means more of your visitors turn into actual leads.

Custom Coded Websites Are More Secure

WordPress and the other builders come with a ton of weak spots that hackers love to poke at. With WordPress especially, every day you skip a plugin update or version patch is another day you’re inviting trouble. That’s why it’s the most targeted platform out there. It’s simply the easiest way in, especially on sites that haven’t been touched in months.

Our setup doesn’t have any of that to worry about. No database, no server-side logic, no admin login page. Just static HTML and CSS files being served up fast. There’s nothing there to hack. We could let one of our sites sit untouched for a decade and it’d still be running just fine. You can’t get much safer than that.

Improve Your Search Ranking

A fast site helps your Google rankings in a couple of ways. The most straightforward one is speed on its own. Google has come right out and said that when two sites have similar content and authority, the one with better PageSpeed Scores and Core Web Vitals comes out ahead.

The other way is a bit more indirect. Faster sites keep more visitors around, and that gives Google more signals showing that people actually like what they’re seeing. By focusing on Core Web Vitals, we build a site that keeps people engaged longer, with better session times and more clicks through the pages. All of that tells Google you’re the kind of result people want, and your rankings go up because of it.
