We Make Websites With Purpose & Intent

My name is Manny, and I'm the owner and lead developer of PeakRanking Digital Solutions. I got into programming because I wanted a career I could do from anywhere. I grew up in Marietta, GA, and the idea of being tied to a single office for the next 40 years never really sat right with me. I wanted to build something I could run online, travel the world with, and still deliver great work no matter where I was that week. So over the course of two years I taught myself how to code websites the right way by hand, and I started reaching out to small startups to help them with their websites and SEO.

Here's what I noticed pretty quickly. Most small businesses just don't have five or ten thousand dollars sitting around to drop on a new website. And the ones that do? A lot of them get burned by some agency that cashes the check, hands over a clunky template that looks like it came out of 2014, and disappears. Either way, the owner ends up stuck. They either can't afford quality work, or they can but have no clue who to trust with it. So I built a business model that fits how small businesses actually spend. That's where the $0 down and $150 a month plan came from. It's easy to budget for, it doesn't drain the bank account upfront, and it gets the owner a site that actually competes.

Our Working Process

We Take Care Of The Whole Thing

Once the contract is signed, we send over a short questionnaire about your business, then we expand on that content, design the site, get your approval, and build. Most projects go from signed contract to live site in 1 to 2 weeks.

Step 1

Answer A Few Questions

We send a short email with fewer than 10 questions about your business, what you offer, what makes you different, and who your customers are. We take it from there.

Step 2

Review The Design

We hop on a video screen share and walk you through the first draft of your site together. We tweak anything you want changed until you're 100% satisfied with the design.

Step 3

Build And Launch

Once the design is approved, we hand code the site and set up everything that goes with it. Hosting, analytics, Search Console, the works. You get a private demo link to review before we flip the switch and go live.
