Google Business Profile Is The Foundation

If you only do one thing for your small business online, make it your Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business). This is the listing that shows up when someone searches your business name, or searches “[your service] near me” and sees the map with three results at the top. Most small businesses have a half-finished profile with the wrong hours, no photos, and zero posts. That’s not just a missed opportunity. It’s actively hurting your rankings.

We handle the full setup and ongoing management. Complete profile optimization with every field filled in, weekly photo uploads showing your actual work, regular posts to signal to Google that you’re an active business, Q&A monitoring so you’re answering common questions before your customers even call, and review management to build the 5-star social proof that converts lookers into callers.

Citations And Directories, The Unsexy Work That Matters

Google trusts businesses that have consistent information across the internet. Your name, address, and phone number (NAP) need to match exactly across Yelp, Facebook, Bing Places, Apple Maps, Yellow Pages, industry-specific directories, and dozens of smaller sites that nobody thinks about but Google definitely notices. One wrong address on Yellow Pages can tank your local ranking.

This is tedious, boring, time-consuming work. Nobody wants to do it, which is exactly why it’s so valuable when we do it for you. We’ll audit your current citations, fix the inconsistencies, and build new ones in the directories that actually matter for your industry.

How Long Does It Take?

Here’s the honest answer: 3 to 6 months to see meaningful ranking movement. Anyone who tells you they can get you to #1 in 30 days is either lying or about to get you penalized. Google intentionally moves slowly on local rankings because they want to see consistent signals over time, not quick manipulation attempts.

Month 1 is setup: audit, fixes, Google Business optimization, initial citations. Month 2-3 you’ll start seeing your profile in more searches and some initial ranking improvements. Month 4-6 is where the compound effect kicks in. More reviews, more citations, more content, and rankings start climbing for more competitive keywords. By month 6-12, most clients are ranking in the top 3 for their primary local keywords and seeing a meaningful increase in calls.
