What Local SEO Actually Is (And How It Differs From Regular SEO)

If you serve a specific geographic area, traditional SEO is only half the story. What you need is local SEO: optimizing your online presence so you earn more business from relevant local searches happening in your city right now.

When someone types an “X in Y” query, such as “accountant in Chicago” or “roof repair near me,” Google applies a different rule set than it does for broad national queries. Local SEO versus general SEO is about the surface you want to win. Regular SEO emphasizes global or national ranking through content and links. Local SEO emphasizes proximity, your real-world location, review patterns, and the quality of your Google Business Profile. To win someone five miles away, you need a local strategy, not a generic one. For a dedicated explainer, read our beginner’s guide to what local SEO is.

How Local Search Has Changed in 2026, and What It Means for Your Business

Local search in 2026 is not the same as even a year or two ago. Many owners notice fewer calls from “standard” listings because the SERP itself is changing. Google has been testing AI local packs on mobile, expanding Local Services Ads, and shifting click behavior. We do not pretend nothing has changed; planning around the new landscape is part of the job.

The rough shape of that shift:

  • A meaningful share of US mobile queries now show AI-style local packs instead of only classic layouts.
  • Local Services Ads appear on a growing fraction of queries compared with prior years.
  • New AI pack formats can surface fewer distinct businesses than the traditional 3-pack, which makes strong fundamentals even more valuable.

Local SEO is not disappearing; it is evolving. Businesses that ignored technical and review foundations feel the squeeze first. Practices with solid local SEO are better positioned to earn visibility as layouts change, including AI-driven surfaces that reward structured, entity-clear websites.

Frequently Asked Questions About Local SEO

How long does local SEO take?

Google Maps visibility typically improves within 60 to 90 days of Google Business Profile optimization and citation building. Organic rankings for competitive local keywords usually move meaningfully between months 3 and 6. A full 12-month program captures maximum market share across all three visibility surfaces: Maps, organic search, and AI-powered local results.

What is the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?

Regular SEO focuses on ranking pages globally or nationally based on broad keywords, backlinks, and content depth. Local SEO targets searchers in a specific geographic area. It relies heavily on proximity to the searcher, your Google Business Profile, local citations, and customer reviews to rank you in the Map Pack and localized organic results.

Is local SEO worth it for a small business?

Yes, especially compared to paid advertising. Google Ads generate traffic only while your budget runs. Local SEO builds rankings that generate leads indefinitely. A well-optimized local presence compounds over time: each month of consistent optimization adds to a permanent asset that competitors cannot easily displace.

Local SEO vs Google Ads: which is better?

Both serve different functions. Google Ads deliver immediate, controllable visibility but stop the moment your budget runs out. Local SEO builds durable rankings that generate leads at zero marginal cost once established. The most successful small businesses use both: ads for immediate lead flow while SEO builds the permanent organic foundation that makes ads less necessary over time.

How do I rank higher on Google Maps as a small business?

Google Maps rankings are driven by three factors: relevance (how closely your GBP matches what the searcher wants), distance (proximity to the searcher), and prominence (reviews, citations, and website trust signals). High-impact moves include choosing the most specific GBP category you can justify, building a steady weekly flow of detailed reviews, and keeping business information consistent across directories.
