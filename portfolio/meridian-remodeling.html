Our Process

A Process Built
for Clarity.

Remodeling anxiety comes from uncertainty. Our process is designed to eliminate uncertainty at every step — so you always know exactly where your project stands.

01
Discovery Consultation

We meet at your home, discuss your vision, lifestyle, and budget. No pressure, no templates — we listen first. Complimentary, always.

02
Concept & 3D Design

Your designer develops a custom concept with 3D renderings, material boards, and a preliminary budget. You approve every detail before we move forward.

03
Fixed-Price Proposal

A detailed written proposal with every line item priced. This is the number you pay — unless you request changes. No contingency markups, no surprise additions.

04
Build & Communication

Daily photo updates, weekly calls with your project manager. We treat your home with the same care we'd want for our own families.

05
Reveal & Warranty

Final walkthrough together. Your 5-year warranty begins the day you move back in. We're a phone call away if anything needs attention.
