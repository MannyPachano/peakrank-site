What we teach

Every style.
Every level.

Vinyasa flow

Breath-led movement connecting posture to posture in a continuous, meditative flow. Builds strength, flexibility, and focus.

All levels

Hatha basics

The perfect starting point. Slower-paced with detailed instruction on foundational poses, breathing, and alignment.

Beginner friendly

Yin & restorative

Long-held postures that target deep connective tissue. The antidote to a fast-paced life. No experience needed.

All levels

Power yoga

Athletic, strength-focused sequences that build serious endurance and heat. Challenging but always accessible.

Intermediate+

Prenatal yoga

Safe, nurturing practice designed for every trimester. Builds strength for birth and community with other expectant mothers.

All trimesters

Sound bath & meditation

Bi-weekly sessions using singing bowls and guided meditation for deep rest and nervous system reset.

About the studio

Built on the belief
that everyone belongs

Root & Rise was founded in 2017 by Amara Sterling after a decade of practice that transformed her life following a serious back injury. She built the studio she always wished existed — zero judgment, genuine community, and teaching that meets you exactly where you are.

Our six teachers bring diverse backgrounds and styles — but share the same commitment to making every student feel seen and supported, whether it's their first class or their five-hundredth.
