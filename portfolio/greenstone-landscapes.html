Our Approach

We Design For
How You Live Outdoors

Most landscapers give you what they know how to build. We start with how you use your outdoor space — then design around that. Every project is different because every family is different.

Native plant expertise
We specialize in Texas-native and drought-tolerant plants that thrive in Central Texas heat — no constant watering required.
3D design before we dig
You'll see a photorealistic rendering of your finished landscape before we break ground. What you approve is what we build.
One team, start to finish
Your designer is on-site during installation. No handoffs, no miscommunication — the person who drew it builds it.
The Greenstone GuaranteeAll plantings are guaranteed to establish in their first season. If something doesn't take, we replace it — free of charge, no questions asked.
