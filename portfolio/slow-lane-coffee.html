Our story

A slower kind of coffee shop

Slow Lane started as a tiny 400 sq ft space with one espresso machine and a bench out front. Seven years later the bench is still there — and so are most of the regulars. We built this café around the idea that a good cup of coffee deserves your full attention. Not a lid. Not a rush.

01
Sourced with intention
We work with three small-batch roasters, all with direct farmer relationships and transparent pricing.
02
Baked fresh every morning
Everything in the pastry case is made from scratch in our kitchen starting at 5:30am. When it's gone, it's gone.
03
Community first
Monthly cupping events, local artist shows, and a bulletin board that's older than most of our customers.
