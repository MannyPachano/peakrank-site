Common questions

How does the 90-day traffic guarantee work?

For Local and Enterprise growth plans, if agreed organic traffic hasn’t grown by at least 15% within 90 days from the baseline we set together, we keep executing the growth plan at no fee until that threshold is met. Baseline, measurement source, and scope are confirmed in writing at onboarding.

Do you use shortcuts or risky tactics?

No way. We stick to ethical, proven SEO strategies. By focusing on quality content, technical excellence, and genuine authority building, we ensure your rankings are not only strong but also sustainable.

What’s parasite SEO?

Parasite SEO is about leveraging high-authority platforms like Medium or LinkedIn to rank content quickly. These platforms already have search engine trust, so your optimized content gets visibility faster, driving traffic back to your website.

Why do I need a 4-month commitment for the maintenance plan?

The first few months of SEO are critical for building a strong foundation. Audits, site structure, authority building, and backlinks all take time. Once that foundation is set, the maintenance plan ensures you keep your rankings while continuing to improve what’s already there.

Should I add the website redesign?

If your website is outdated, slow, or struggling to convert traffic into leads, the redesign is a smart move. Even the best SEO in the world won’t help if your site can’t hold visitors’ attention or drive conversions. The $1,500 add-on covers up to 10 pre-existing pages; larger sites get a custom quote after we review what you need.
